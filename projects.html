慢门摄影 · Slow Shutter Photography

慢门摄影并不是简单地延长曝光时间，而是一种重新理解时间与光线的方式。 当快门变慢，水流变得柔软，云层开始移动，城市的灯光被时间拉长。 在这种影像之中，瞬间不再只是一个瞬间，而成为时间缓慢展开的痕迹。

Slow shutter photography is not merely about using a longer exposure. It is a way of observing how time and light interact within a scene. When the shutter slows down, water becomes fluid, clouds begin to drift, and city lights stretch across the frame.
