Photography Works

精选摄影作品集，侧重呈现单张作品的视觉与气质。
A curated gallery of selected works — focused on single images and their mood.

多瑙河畔 · 古堡与光

Danube Castle — Light in the Mist

云雾掠过山坡，古老城堡像记忆一样缓慢浮现。
A castle emerges slowly, as if memory rises through the mist.

南澳沙丘 · 光影的纹理

South Australian Dunes — Texture of Light

风写下沙的曲线，光在起伏之间完成雕刻。
Wind draws the lines; light sculpts the dunes in silence.

梦莲湖 · 静水与群峰

Lake Moraine — Peaks and Still Water

湖面安静得像一面镜子，把群山的呼吸收进蓝色里。
A still lake holds the mountains’ breath in deep blue.

黄金海岸 · 城市的慢门

Gold Coast — A Slow City

水面拉平了喧嚣，城市在长曝光里变得从容。
The water smooths the noise; the city becomes calm in long exposure.

新加坡 · 光束与海湾

Singapore — Beams over the Bay

光从建筑间射出，夜色像舞台，城市像一场演出。
Light cuts through the skyline; the night turns into a stage.

悉尼 · 港湾的时间痕

Sydney — Traces of Time

桥与灯火在水面写下时间，色彩被拉长成一条河。
Bridge lights stretch into a river of time across the water.
