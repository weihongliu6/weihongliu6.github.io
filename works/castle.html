Danube Castle — Light in the Mist

多瑙河 · 古堡与光
Danube Castle — Light in the Mist
地点：德国 · 多瑙河沿岸 关键词：雾 / 光 / 记忆 风格：慢门氛围

云雾掠过山坡，古堡像一段被时间保留的句子，缓慢浮现。 我更愿意把这张照片理解为“记忆的显影”：光并不急于照亮一切， 它只是轻轻提示——有些存在，本就该以缓慢的方式被看见。

Mist drifts over the hillside, and the castle appears like a sentence preserved by time. I see this image as a kind of developing process: light doesn’t rush to reveal everything— it simply suggests that some presences are meant to be seen slowly.
